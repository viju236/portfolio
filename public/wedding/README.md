## Quick Setup

1. Take or edit two versions of your couple photo:
   - One in portrait orientation (tall)
   - One in landscape orientation (wide)

2. Save them in this folder as:
   - `Vertical.JPG`
   - `Horizontal.JPG`

3. Refresh your browser!
